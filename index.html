Lake Dubay is in central Wisconsin and every year, countless dedicated fishermen flock here once the first layer of ice forms over the lake. Instead of making a pilgrimmage to the lake, visitors rely on this ice report to know when they can safely walk on the lake with their fishing supplies. the fishing outlook, and other relevant information to fishing out on the great Lake Dubay.
